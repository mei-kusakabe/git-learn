What is E-PIN ?
E-Pin is an innovative payment system that allows MLM recruits and businesses to enable a highly secured option to gather payment. Prime MLM software helps MLM businesses to generate a secured e-pin for any significant transactions and upgradations. A highly secured e-pin can be used for a range of options, such as for adding recruits, upgrading the current program, and for reinstating participants.
